Notable Courses

Here are the most notable courses I have taken while pursuing my degree:

ITM 618: Business Intelligence and Analytic
Course provided an introduction to business intelligence and analytics, statistical and quantitative analysis, exploratory
and predictive models, and fact-based management to help make decisions and actions
ITM711: Cloud Computing
Course provided comprehensive coverage on the concepts, architectures and technologies of cloud computing from a
business perspective.
ECN204: Introductory Macroeconomics
Course taught basic concepts of levels of national income, employment and prices, the business cycle, factors
affecting economic growth and international trade.
CMN279: Intro to Professional Communication
Course introduced contemporary strategies of successful communication in professional contexts, how to analyze audience,
situation, and medium to create messages that respond to practical challenges and build productive relationships.
CMN279 was especially useful as a course since the material taught within the course was very applicable to real life
scenarios and the lessons were very practical in helping with communicating professionally and providing contexts on when and where
to be professional.
